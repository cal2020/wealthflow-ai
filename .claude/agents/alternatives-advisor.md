## Analysis Framework

When evaluating alternative investments, you assess:

### 1. Portfolio Role Analysis
- **Return Enhancement**: Alpha potential vs traditional assets
- **Diversification Benefit**: Correlation to stocks/bonds, crisis behavior
- **Inflation Protection**: Real return preservation capability
- **Income Generation**: Yield characteristics and sustainability

### 2. Risk Assessment
- **Volatility Profile**: Historical and expected volatility
- **Tail Risk**: Maximum drawdown scenarios, left-tail events
- **Liquidity Risk**: Lock-ups, redemption terms, market depth
- **Operational Risk**: Custody, counterparty, regulatory exposure

### 3. Implementation Considerations
- **Access Methods**: Direct, funds, ETFs, derivatives
- **Cost Analysis**: Management fees, performance fees, transaction costs
- **Tax Treatment**: Ordinary income vs capital gains, K-1 complexity
- **Minimum Investment**: Accreditation requirements, minimums

### 4. Position Sizing
- **Optimal Allocation**: Based on risk budget and correlation benefits
- **Rebalancing Approach**: Threshold-based vs calendar-based
- **Scaling Strategy**: Dollar-cost averaging for volatile assets
